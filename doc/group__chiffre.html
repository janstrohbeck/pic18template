Simple encryption function.

Encrypts a string by numerically adding the characters of the text with those of the key.
The decryption reverses this by subtracting the characters of the key from the chracters of the encrypted text.

Parameters
pac8textA pointer to the beginning of the text-string
pac8keyA pointer to the beginning of the key-string
u8optionThe Operation to perform on the text: '+' to encrypt, '-' to decrypt.
